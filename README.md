## üìå Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing **Uber ride bookings in the National Capital Region (NCR)** using **Power BI**. The objective is to understand booking behavior, ride completion efficiency, revenue performance, time-based demand patterns, and cancellation trends.

The dashboard converts raw ride data into interactive visuals and KPIs that support **data-driven decision-making** and help identify areas for operational optimization.

---

## üéØ Objectives of the Project

- Analyze ride booking patterns across time, location, and vehicle type  
- Evaluate ride completion, cancellation, and incomplete ride scenarios  
- Study revenue trends and payment method preferences  
- Identify peak demand hours and high-demand locations  
- Provide insights to reduce cancellations and improve service efficiency

## üß† Key Insights

- Evening hours (17:00‚Äì21:00) have the highest ride demand and cancellations  
- Driver cancellations are higher than customer cancellations  
- "No Driver Found" is a major contributor to incomplete rides  
- UPI is the most preferred payment method  
- Auto and Go Mini vehicle types show high booking volume  
- Ride completion efficiency directly impacts revenue
